# Contributing

Contributions are welcome if they keep the repository generic, public-safe, and useful for actuaries and AI agents.

## Rules

- Do not submit secrets, credentials, customer data, claim data, policy data, or internal company data.
- Keep regulatory notes source-linked and jurisdiction/date-aware.
- Do not present templates as legal, actuarial, insurance, security, or compliance advice.
- Prefer checklists, playbooks, templates, and guardrails over company-specific implementation details.
- Keep examples synthetic and clearly marked as examples.

## Suggested Contribution Types

- Generic actuarial playbooks.
- Data quality checklists.
- Insurance analytics templates.
- Agent instruction templates.
- Security/privacy guardrails.
- Links to official public regulatory sources.
